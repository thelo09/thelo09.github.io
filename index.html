Research Areas

Radio Access Networks

Embracing the paradigm shift in network architecture, Networks Lab pioneers research in Open RAN (Radio Access Network). By liberating the traditionally monolithic and vendor-specific RAN, we champion an open, modular, and interoperable ecosystem. Our efforts focus on creating flexible and customizable network infrastructures, empowering network operators to embrace multi-vendor environments, foster innovation, and drive cost efficiencies. Through experimentation and prototyping, we shape the future of network virtualization, orchestration, and management.

The Fifth Generation Networks

Welcome to the realm of 5G, where the Networks Lab at IIT Delhi is at the forefront of spearheading groundbreaking research and development. In a world that demands faster, more reliable, and immersive connectivity, our lab is dedicated to exploring the boundless potential of the fifth generation of wireless technology. Prepare to delve into the extraordinary world of 5G as we unravel its transformative capabilities and the impact it will have on industries, societies, and individuals alike.
